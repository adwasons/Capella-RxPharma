Pharmacogenomic Considerations

⚠ CYP2C19 Gene Impact
Patients with reduced-function CYP2C19 alleles may have decreased drug activation, leading to reduced antiplatelet effect and increased risk of cardiovascular events. Approximately 30% of the population carries at least one reduced-function allele.
✓ CPIC Recommendation Level A
Intermediate / Poor Metabolizers: Consider alternative antiplatelet therapy (e.g., prasugrel or ticagrelor) if no contraindication.
Normal / Rapid Metabolizers: Standard dosing of clopidogrel is appropriate.
Ultra-rapid Metabolizers: Standard dosing; monitor for bleeding.
